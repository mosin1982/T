\# Compliance Positioning



\*\*Project:\*\* T

\*\*Maintainer:\*\* T Technology Research Lab

\*\*Status:\*\* Public Alpha / Research Software



T is designed as a compliance-aware, research-only Financial Intelligence Operating System. It is not a regulated financial advisory product, broker, exchange, portfolio manager, or trade execution service.



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```



\## 1. Purpose



The purpose of this document is to define how T should be positioned publicly, commercially, and technically so that users understand its intended role.



T should be presented as:



\* Research infrastructure

\* Backtesting software

\* Dashboard analytics software

\* Safety-guarded research tooling

\* Educational and experimental public alpha software

\* Human-reviewed financial research support



T should not be presented as:



\* Financial advisory software

\* Investment advisory software

\* Return-assurance software

\* Direct trade instruction software

\* Portfolio management software

\* Broker replacement

\* Exchange replacement

\* Fully regulated finance product



\## 2. Core Compliance Position



T provides infrastructure for studying data, running simulations, reviewing metrics, and generating research-oriented outputs.



T does not decide whether a user should buy, sell, hold, trade, invest, or allocate capital.



T does not determine whether any user is legally allowed to access, analyze, trade, or act in any market.



Users are responsible for understanding and following all rules that apply to them.



\## 3. Required Disclaimer



All major public-facing documentation, dashboards, generated reports, and client-facing descriptions should include or align with:



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```



Longer form:



```text

T is research-only software. It does not provide financial advice, investment advice, trading recommendations, portfolio management, or legal/tax/compliance advice.

```



\## 4. Safe Public Positioning



Recommended description:



```text

T is a research-only Financial Intelligence Operating System for studying market data through backtesting, dashboard analytics, risk review, and safety-guarded research workflows.

```



Recommended commercial description:



```text

T Technology Research Lab may provide paid setup, customization, dashboard configuration, data integration, training, deployment assistance, and technical support for T. T remains research-only software and does not provide financial advice or trading recommendations.

```



\## 5. Unsafe Positioning To Avoid



Avoid public, commercial, dashboard, CLI, and report language such as:



\* Direct buy or sell instructions

\* Return-assurance claims

\* Risk-free claims

\* Overconfident market prediction language

\* Advisory relationship claims

\* Managed investment claims

\* Fully compliant for all markets

\* Production-ready trading system

\* Autonomous trading decision system



\## 6. Human Review Requirement



T outputs should be reviewed by a human before being used in:



\* Research reports

\* Client demos

\* Public posts

\* Business decisions

\* Market commentary

\* Training sessions

\* Technical documentation

\* Investment-related discussion



Human review is required because data, models, assumptions, and market conditions may be incomplete, outdated, or incorrect.



\## 7. Market Responsibility



Market rules and requirements vary by:



\* Country

\* Exchange

\* Broker

\* Asset class

\* Instrument type

\* Data provider

\* Tax jurisdiction

\* User category



T does not verify user eligibility, regulatory permissions, broker permissions, tax obligations, or exchange access rights.



Users are responsible for their own compliance obligations.



\## 8. Data Provider Responsibility



Users are responsible for checking:



\* Data license terms

\* API usage rules

\* Redistribution restrictions

\* Commercial usage restrictions

\* Rate limits

\* Exchange data requirements

\* Broker/data provider agreements



T should not imply that all data can be freely used for all purposes.



\## 9. Deployment Positioning



T may be deployed locally, on a private server, or in a controlled environment for research purposes.



Deployment support should be described as:



\* Guided deployment support

\* Setup assistance

\* Configuration support

\* Dashboard launch support

\* Troubleshooting support

\* Documentation-based handover



Avoid describing deployment as:



\* Fully managed financial production service

\* Guaranteed compliance deployment

\* Fully regulated trading infrastructure

\* Managed investment technology



\## 10. Donation and Service Boundaries



Donations are voluntary support for development.



Donations do not create:



\* Investment relationship

\* Advisory relationship

\* Trading promise

\* Return promise

\* Service obligation

\* Support SLA

\* Portfolio management relationship



Paid services must be scoped separately and should focus on technical setup, customization, training, deployment assistance, integration, and support.



\## 11. Safety Guard Positioning



T may include a hallucination-resistant research guard.



This guard is designed to reduce unsafe financial wording, but it does not make the system legally compliant for every market or jurisdiction.



Correct positioning:



```text

The safety guard helps reduce unsafe research output language.

```



Avoid:



```text

The safety guard guarantees legal compliance.

```



\## 12. AI/ML Positioning



If AI/ML features are added, they should be positioned as:



\* Research intelligence

\* Adaptive research assistance

\* Human-reviewed analysis support

\* Explainable research reporting

\* Pattern-learning support

\* Risk observation support



Avoid:



\* Autonomous financial advisor

\* Self-directed investment brain

\* Fully automated trading decision engine

\* Profit-prediction AI

\* Legal/compliance replacement



\## 13. Production Readiness Position



Current public alpha positioning:



```text

T is public alpha research software.

```



Safer commercial wording:



```text

Production deployment assistance may be provided for research environments, subject to scope, security review, and client responsibility.

```



Avoid:



```text

T is guaranteed production-ready financial trading software.

```



\## 14. Official Compliance Statement



Recommended official statement:



```text

T provides research infrastructure only. It does not provide financial advice, investment advice, trading recommendations, portfolio management, legal advice, tax advice, compliance advice, broker services, exchange services, or default execution services. Users are responsible for human review and for following all applicable laws, regulations, exchange rules, broker terms, tax rules, and data-provider terms.

```



\## 15. Disclaimer



T remains research-only software.



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```
