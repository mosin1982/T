\# Support Scope



\*\*Project:\*\* T

\*\*Maintainer:\*\* T Technology Research Lab

\*\*Status:\*\* Public Alpha / Research Software



T Technology Research Lab may provide guided technical support for T. Support must remain clearly scoped, documentation-based, and research-only.



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```



\## 1. Purpose



The purpose of this document is to define what support may include, what support does not include, and how support should be communicated to users, clients, donors, and contributors.



T is a public alpha research project. Support should be realistic, limited-scope, and transparent.



\## 2. Support Position



T support should be described as:



\* Guided setup support

\* Technical configuration support

\* Dashboard launch support

\* Documentation walkthrough

\* Troubleshooting support

\* Research workflow assistance

\* Deployment guidance for research environments



T support should not be described as:



\* Managed financial service

\* Investment advisory support

\* Trading decision support

\* Portfolio management support

\* Regulated compliance guarantee

\* 24/7 emergency production support unless separately agreed



\## 3. Included Support



Support may include:



\* GitHub repository setup guidance

\* Python environment setup

\* Dependency installation

\* Local dashboard startup

\* Backtest demo guidance

\* Basic troubleshooting

\* Documentation walkthrough

\* Dashboard customization guidance

\* Report customization guidance

\* Data integration planning

\* Deployment assistance for research use

\* General technical Q\&A



\## 4. Excluded Support



Support does not include:



\* Financial advice

\* Investment advice

\* Trading recommendations

\* Buy/sell decisions

\* Portfolio management

\* Tax advice

\* Legal advice

\* Regulatory compliance advice

\* Broker account management

\* Exchange account management

\* Data-provider legal review

\* Return assurance

\* Managed trading services



\## 5. Deployment Support



Deployment support may include:



\* Local setup guidance

\* VPS setup guidance

\* Docker setup guidance

\* Environment variable guidance

\* Dashboard launch guidance

\* Basic log review

\* Restart process guidance

\* Documentation-based handover



Deployment support does not mean T Technology Research Lab fully manages the user's production environment.



Users remain responsible for:



\* Server security

\* Access control

\* Backups

\* Credentials

\* Firewall rules

\* Compliance review

\* Data-provider terms

\* Broker/exchange terms

\* Human review of outputs



\## 6. Small-Team Support Model



T Technology Research Lab may operate with a small team.



Support should therefore be:



\* Checklist-based

\* Documentation-based

\* Repeatable

\* Limited in scope

\* Scheduled when needed

\* Not advertised as unlimited emergency support



Recommended support model:



```text

Guided Deployment Support

```



This means T Technology Research Lab may guide users through setup, configuration, launch, and troubleshooting steps.

\## 9. Communication Rule



Support communication should be clear and safe.



Recommended wording:



```text

T Technology Research Lab provides guided technical support for setup, configuration, dashboard launch, troubleshooting, and documentation-based handover. T remains research-only software and does not provide financial advice, trading recommendations, portfolio management, or managed investment services.

```



Avoid wording such as:



```text

We manage your trading system.

We provide financial decisions.

We guarantee market outcomes.

We provide regulated advisory services.

```



\## 10. Client Responsibility



Clients and users are responsible for:



\* Providing accurate requirements

\* Reviewing outputs

\* Securing credentials

\* Managing hosting access

\* Understanding local laws

\* Following exchange and broker rules

\* Following data-provider terms

\* Making their own decisions

\* Getting legal/tax/compliance advice where required



\## 11. Donation Support Boundary



Donations are voluntary.



A donation does not automatically create:



\* Support entitlement

\* Service obligation

\* SLA

\* Advisory relationship

\* Trading promise

\* Deployment responsibility

\* Priority support guarantee



Paid support should be agreed separately.



\## 12. Issue Reporting



Users should report technical issues with:



\* Operating system

\* Python version

\* Command used

\* Error message

\* Screenshot if useful

\* Relevant logs

\* Expected behavior

\* Actual behavior



Users should not share:



\* Passwords

\* API keys

\* Private keys

\* Seed phrases

\* Recovery codes

\* Broker credentials

\* Exchange credentials
