# Donation Ethics

Donation requests must be respectful and optional.

Good positioning:

> If T helps your research, learning, or development, you may support future work.

Avoid:

- Emotional pressure
- Fake urgency
- Profit guarantees
- Claims like "donate to unlock profits"
- Misleading performance screenshots
- Asking users to send private keys or seed phrases

Recommended wording:

T is free for personal, educational, and research use. If the project saves you time or helps you learn, donations help fund infrastructure, data APIs, documentation, and open-source maintenance.
